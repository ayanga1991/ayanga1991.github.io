I am 4th year Computer Science Ph.D student at the National University of Singapore advised by Professor Li-Shiuan Peh. Before joining NUS, I worked as Research Engineer in SYNERGEN Technology Labs, a USA-based startup from 2016-2019. I was graduated from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka with BSc. Eng. (Hons) specialized in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering in 2016.

My research involves improving the privacy and security of wearable devices and also data collected from wearable devices. I am also interested on Wearable computing/sensing, wearable energy harvesting and wearable power and latency optimization.
